Nunawading Kangas Basketball Club is a community based junior club established over 50 years ago. Our players are drawn from Nunawading and the surrounding suburbs. Our aim is to provide an environment for our players to enjoy sport whilst improving their skills, learning about teamwork and respect for other players, coaches and referees. Older players have the opportunity to develop leadership skills through coaching younger teams.

We are part of the Melbourne East Basketball Association (MEBA) and have over 800 players (over 90 teams) playing in the Junior Domestic Competitions.

## Our Mission

To be premier facilitators of participation, development and excellence in basketball.

## Our Purpose

- To select and provide teams to play competitive basketball with other teams and clubs.
- To teach basketball skills, tactics and rules to its members.
- To provide coaching and adequate training facilities for its members.
- To foster and maintain at all times, the highest level of sportsmanship and fair play.
- To co-operate with similar clubs to promote and improve the game of basketball.
- To further the aim of affiliated basketball association(s) to which we belong.
- To provide and promote a range of social activities amongst its membership.
- To provide and promote any other sporting activity that shall benefit the community.

## Our Values

To promote the following values:

- Enjoyment of basketball
- Honesty and Integrity
- To respect all, regardless of gender, ability, cultural background or religion
- Understanding
- Openess and Transparency
- Teamwork and Co-operation

## Life Members

Since our formation in 1974, our Club has been run by a small crew of dedicated volunteers drawn from the families of our players. Many of these volunteers have made significant contributions, far in excess of what was expected of them and over extended years of involvement. Without their involvement, the Club would not have been able to continue to provide the opportunity for kids to play basketball.

The Club recognises their much appreciated involvment by bestowing Life Member status on them.
