## Partner with a proud local basketball community

Nunawading Kangas Basketball Club is home to a large, active and loyal junior basketball community. With hundreds of players across our teams, plus parents, carers, siblings, grandparents, coaches, team managers and volunteers, sponsorship is a practical way to place your business in front of local families who are regularly engaged with the Club.

If you are looking to advertise to approximately 650+ kids and their families, your business could be seen by a broad community audience while also supporting one of the area's long-standing junior sporting clubs.

## Why sponsor the Kangas?

Sponsorship is more than a logo placement. It is a chance to build positive local recognition with families who value community involvement, kids' sport, teamwork and healthy participation. When a business supports junior basketball, people notice. It shows that your organisation is invested in the people and places around it.

Your sponsorship can help the Club continue to provide a welcoming, well-run basketball experience for young players. Support from sponsors can contribute to areas such as player development, coaching resources, training equipment, uniforms, events and the everyday costs of running a volunteer-led club.

For your business, it is an opportunity to:

- Increase awareness with local families throughout the basketball season.
- Show visible support for grassroots junior sport.
- Build goodwill with parents, players, coaches and community volunteers.
- Associate your brand with participation, teamwork, resilience and positive youth development.
- Reach an audience that is local, connected and highly engaged.

## A strong marketing opportunity

Families involved in junior sport are active in their local area. They attend training, games, events and club activities, and they often support businesses that support their children and their community. Sponsoring the Kangas gives your organisation a meaningful point of connection with this audience.

Whether you are a local business, professional service, retailer, trade, health provider or larger organisation wanting to strengthen community ties, sponsorship can help keep your name front of mind with people who live, work, study and play in the area.

## Your details could go here

Your business details could be featured through Club sponsorship opportunities and promoted to a wide audience across the Nunawading Kangas community. It is a positive way to advertise while making a real contribution to the Club and the young players who represent it each week.

To discuss sponsorship opportunities, please contact the Secretary at [Secretary@nunawadingkangas.com.au](mailto:Secretary@nunawadingkangas.com.au).
